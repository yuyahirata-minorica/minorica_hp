Established Nagoya Branch at "STATION Ai", Japan's Largest Startup Support Hub

MINORICA Inc. (Headquarters: Bunkyo-ku, Tokyo; CEO: Yuya Hirata) is pleased to announce the establishment of our Nagoya Branch on November 1, 2025, at "STATION Ai," the largest startup support hub in Japan, newly opened in Tsurumai, Nagoya City.

Background and Objectives

"STATION Ai" is a massive innovation hub where domestic and international startups, enterprises, investors, and support organizations gather to foster open innovation.
While we have already been active in the Aichi region, moving into STATION Ai will allow us to strengthen regional collaboration.

Nagoya Branch Overview

Location:

Nagoya, Aichi 466-0064, Japan

We remain committed to our mission of "Strengthening plants, accelerating agriculture" and will continue to push forward with our research, development, and business expansion.
