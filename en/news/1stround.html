Selected for "1stRound", Japan's largest technology startup support program

MINORICA Inc. has been selected for the "1stRound" program, a comprehensive startup support program co-hosted by university-affiliated venture capitals.

1stRound is one of the largest incubation programs in Japan, providing hands-on support for deep-tech startups from universities and research institutions. Through this selection, MINORICA will receive support in business development, fundraising, and collaboration with partner companies.

Future Outlook

With the support of the 1stRound program, we will further accelerate the research and development of our plant vaccine technology and establish a solid business foundation for social implementation.
