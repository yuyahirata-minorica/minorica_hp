OUR TECHNOLOGY

Plant Enhancer "PLANT-CALL"

What are plant enhancers?

Plant-enhancers are innovative agricultural materials that promote crop growth by activating the plant's immune properties from the germination stage. Unlike chemical fertilizers, they do not harm the environment.

Our alcohol-based enhancers are particularly effective for grains and fruits. With low viscosity, they can be rapidly sprayed using drones.

Key Features

  • Easy Implementation: Simply mix with irrigation water. A purely organic solution.
  • Quality Improvement: Increases Brix levels, amino acids, and flavor.
  • Stress Resistance: Protects crops against drought, heat, salinity, and disease.
  • High Productivity: Promotes growth for stable, high yields.
